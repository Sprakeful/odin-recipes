Lasagna recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 poung ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 cup spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200deg.
  2. Stir the seasoning and sauce into beef.
  3. Top with 2 noodles
  4. Spread 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  5. Layer it 2 more times
  6. Cover with alufoil, bake 30 mins
